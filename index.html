Shaoqi QI 戚少奇

I am a PhD researcher in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Liverpool, working on 3D computer vision. My research focuses on monocular multi-agent 3D Gaussian Splatting SLAM — enabling multiple cameras to collaboratively reconstruct a single, geometrically and photometrically consistent scene from RGB video alone, without depth sensors. I am especially interested in compact submap communication, geometry- and appearance-aware loop verification, and occupancy-aware Gaussian fusion under monocular scale ambiguity.

MAGS-SLAM: Monocular Multi-Agent Gaussian Splatting SLAM for Geometrically and Photometrically Consistent Reconstruction

Shaoqi QI et al.

Under review, 2026

The first RGB-only multi-agent 3D Gaussian Splatting SLAM for collaborative photorealistic scene reconstruction. We introduce compact submap communication, geometry- and appearance-aware loop verification, and occupancy-aware Gaussian fusion, together with ReplicaMultiAgent Plus — a benchmark for collaborative Gaussian SLAM.
