About

I am a PhD researcher at the University of Liverpool working on 3D computer vision. My current focus is dynamic 3D scene reconstruction — combining recurrent pointmap transformers with 3D Gaussian Splatting in a frozen-backbone, plug-in fashion.

Recent work spans monocular SLAM, multi-agent collaborative mapping, and pose estimation under casual capture. I care about reproducible, modular systems that survive contact with real video.

Outside research: long-distance running, film photography, and (occasionally) cooking experiments that should not be rerun.
