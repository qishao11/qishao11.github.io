Qi Shao

I am pursuing a Ph.D. degree in Control Science and Engineering at Harbin Engineering University. I am currently a jointly trained Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Liverpool. I received my bachelor's degree from the College of Intelligent Systems Science and Engineering, Harbin Engineering University.

My recent research focuses on 3D scene representation and SLAM for multi-robot and embodied AI. I study how robots can build shared spatial memory by connecting geometric mapping, 3D Gaussian Splatting, semantic scene understanding, and foundation-model priors for navigation, manipulation, and collaboration.

I also serve as a Huawei Developer Evangelist, promoting China's domestic AI computing ecosystem through Ascend AI practices for robotics and 3D reconstruction.
